Kathmandu Tour Package

Nepal is the tourist's heaven with its rich culture and a vast heritage, the small kingdom of Nepal is blessed with such astonishing and unique sites that it holds its significant position in naming itself as a best destination for a Unique World heritage tour. The culture of Nepal is a combination of heritage, tradition and novelty. Our tour itinerary Kathmandu at Glance / Kathmandu Tour Package is especially prepared for those who want to spend less time in Nepal due to time constrains and who are interested in unique heritage tour of Nepal and are looking for excellent mountain views. Kathmandu Holidays covers all major Heritage sites inside Kathmandu valley with one night excursion to a popular hill station "Nagarkot" located close to Kathmandu offering an excellent view of Central mountain ranges.
